About

I'm a self taught coder and designer based in New York City! I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive designs. When I'm not coding, tweeting or pushing pixels, you'll find me cooking, working out or making short films.

My expertise is in web design, WordPress development, eCommerce stores and SEO optimization. If you'd like me to get involved with helping your business grow, I'm all ears! Whether you're a student looking to showcase your work, a professional looking to attract clients, or a graphic artist looking to collaborate lets get in touch.

Siin Boutique

Siin Boutique is Online Store for fabulous women clothing and accessories all in one place! Our collections range from bold and beautiful to sweet and dreamy, Siin has contemporary clothing to bring out the fashionista in all of us. We offer a variety of trendy apparel and accessories, so you can mix or match products you use every day.

Our unique styles are made with you in mind. We pick, buy and design our products with obsessive attention to details. We provide you with the most sophisticated, chic and modern styles that we know you'll love. We invest in superior construction and high-end finishes to make your Siin purchase a true representation of affordable luxury.
